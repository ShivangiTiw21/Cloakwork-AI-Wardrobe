# Cloakwork — AI-Powered Wardrobe Styling App

An AI wardrobe assistant that gives instant, personalized outfit recommendations no login, no sign-up, zero friction. Built from scratch in 24 hours at the International Women's Day Hackathon.

## The Problem

Getting dressed shouldn't feel like a chore. Most styling apps require lengthy onboarding, account creation, and endless quizzes before you get any value. That creates a barrier especially for people who just want quick, smart outfit suggestions.

## The Solution

Cloakwork removes every barrier. No login. No account. No onboarding flow. You open it and immediately get AI-powered outfit recommendations. The entire UX was designed around one principle: instant value with zero friction.

## How It Was Built

- **Tool:** Lovable (no-code AI app builder)
- **Timeline:** 24 hours, built from scratch
- **Event:** International Women's Day Hackathon
- **Approach:** No-code, focusing on UX design and product thinking over engineering

## Key Design Decisions

- **No login required** — removing sign-up walls means users get value in seconds, not minutes
- **Mobile-first design** — styled for how people actually browse their wardrobes (on their phones)
- **AI-driven suggestions** — personalized recommendations without requiring a style quiz

## What I Learned

- How to go from zero to working product in 24 hours using no-code tools
- The power of removing friction from user onboarding
- How to scope features ruthlessly when you have a hard deadline
